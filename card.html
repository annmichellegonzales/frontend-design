Koala

Koalas aren’t bears – they’re marsupials!

  • Scientific Name: Phascolarctos cinereus
  • Average Length: 2.5 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Open forest woodlands

The koala or koala bear is an arboreal herbivorous marsupial native to Australia. It is the only extant representative of the family Phascolarctidae and its closest living relatives are the wombats.
